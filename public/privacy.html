STIR | Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy.

As part of your experience with STIR, we’ll ask you for your first name, phone number as well as to connect with your Facebook account. Once you do this, none of your personally identifiable information (aside from first name) will be shared with other participants in the experience. We will not store your full name, Facebook data or keep your phone number in a database after you have participated in the experience.

We will not use your information to contact you in any way outside of the text messages we send during the STIR experience. We will not share your personal data, ie name, phone number or personally identifiable information with any third party.

We are using cookies in order to identify you as a user. We do not share them with any other entity or use them outside the scope of this experiment.

We do not display any paid advertisements on our site.

As we are hosted under NFB, all of NFB’s privacy policies apply and can be accessed in more detail here.
